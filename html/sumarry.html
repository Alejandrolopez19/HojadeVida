Sumarry




I consider myself an analytical person and I like to face new challenges, that is why I constantly set goals and strive to achieve them. I am aware that sometimes they cannot be achieved, that is why I also place special emphasis on determining the factors that make me meet my objectives in order to improve the way I solve problems. I also consider myself a person with a very creative mind and that is why I like to try to solve more complex problems each time. I do not like ritual work where unforeseen events do not arise because I consider that they do not require much creativity and in the long term they can be boring. I really like working with people at all levels and I consider myself a person who learns to get to know people in order to get the best out of each one. I think that the image they have of me is that of a professional and responsible person who knows how to listen as well as contribute ideas.
